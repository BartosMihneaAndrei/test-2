Color theory and shade matching furniture

Digital meeting. Results within 3 days.

The Color theory and shade matching furniture service refers to handing over several resources that match the clients needs. Prior to the mandatory online meeting, the designer will look for the perfect furniture to match the clients needs. This service pack does not include any form of functionality for the products, just the adiministration.

Functionality package

Multiple meetings. Full lenght contract.

The "Functionality package" refers to a complete interior design experience. The interior designer and his team will go to longer lenghts to ensure perfect placement, mathematical positioning and beautiful pieces of furniture. This package also includes wallpapers and detailed decorations.

Functionality package-gold edition

Multiple meetings. Full lenght contract. Exclusive meetings with high-end furniture houses.

The "Functionality package-gold edition" refers to the most exclusive interior design experience in Romania. Together with the designer, the client will visit top-tier fashion and furniture design houses, for the most exquisite and expensive products known to man. This package also includes:

  • Exclusive luxury custom furniture
  • Handmade wallpaper from Germany.
  • Raw crystal decorations (ethically sourced)
